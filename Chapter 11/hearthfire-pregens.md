## Using Pre-Gens

1. **Pick a character** that appeals to you
2. **Fill in the blanks** — names, specifics, connections
3. **Connect to other players** — fill in party connections together
4. **Ready to play** in about 10 minutes

For full character creation from scratch, see the Character Creation Guide.
